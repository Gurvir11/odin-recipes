Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

pasta in cream sauce garnished with parmesan cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fettuccine noodles
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 cups freshly grated Parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to package directions
  2. In a saucepan or skillet over low heat, warm the butter and cream
  3. Season with salt and pepper
  4. Place half of the Parmesan into a large serving bowl
  5. Pour the warm butter/cream mixture over the top
  6. Drain the pasta and immediately pour it into the bowl
  7. Toss a couple of times, and then sprinkle in the other half of the Parmesan
  8. Toss to combine, thinning with pasta water if necessary
  9. Serve immediately as a main course or accompaniment to meat or salad
