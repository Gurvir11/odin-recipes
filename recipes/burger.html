Impossible Burger

Description

burger using plant-based patty

Ingredients

  • 12 Ounce Package Impossibleâ„¢ Beef Made From Plants
  • 1 Cup Mayo
  • 2 Tablespoons Sriracha
  • 2 Tablespoons Minced Pepperoncini
  • 2 Tablespoons Ketchup
  • 2 Tablespoons Dill Pickle Relish
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 3 Slices American Cheese
  • 3 Brioche Buns
  • 3 Leaves Butter Lettuce
  • 6 Slices Tomato
  • 6 Onion Slices
  • Dill Pickle Chips

Steps

  1. Combine the minced pepperoncini with the mayo, ketchup, sriracha, and dill pickle relish
  2. Divide the Impossible beef into 3 patties
  3. Season to taste using salt and pepper
  4. Cook over medium-high heat for 2-3 minutes on each side
  5. Add a slice of cheese on the top and cover until the cheese is melted
  6. Lightly toast the brioche buns
  7. Add a layer of sauce created in step 1 to the buns
  8. Top the buns with the patty, lettuce, tomato, onion, and dill pickle chips
